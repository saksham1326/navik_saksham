Numerical Ability Test

Answer the following questions:

    • 1. A mobile phone originally costs ₹25,000 and is offered at a 20% discount during a sale. What is the sale price?
    • 2. If the price of a laptop increases from ₹45,000 to ₹54,000, what is the percentage increase?
    • 3. A factory produces 1,200 units of a product in 8 days. How many units will it produce in 15 days at the same rate?
    • 4. If a car's fuel efficiency is 15 km per liter, how much fuel is needed to travel 450 km?
    • 5. Solve for x: 3x + 15 = 0
    • 6. What is 25% of ₹1,200?
    • 7. A train travels at an average speed of 90 km/h. How long will it take to cover a distance of 450 km?
    • 8. What is the value of π (pi) rounded to two decimal places?
    • 9. If 10 workers can complete a task in 15 days, how many days will it take for 5 workers to complete the same task, assuming they work at the same rate?
    • 10. What is the sum of the interior angles of a pentagon?
    • 11. If a rectangular field has a length of 50 meters and a width of 30 meters, what is its area?
    • 12. Simplify the expression: 4(x + 5) - 3x
    • 13. What is the probability of drawing an Ace from a standard deck of 52 playing cards?
    • 14. If the exchange rate is ₹75 per USD, how many Indian Rupees will you get for 500 USD?
    • 15. A triangle has angles measuring 50°, 60°, and ___?
